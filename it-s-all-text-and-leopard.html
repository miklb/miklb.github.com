It's All Text and Leopard

22 Dec 2007

{page.summary}

I recently had upgraded to Leopard, and one FireFox extension I’ve come to count on was It’s All Text!. Which allows you to chose a text editor of your choice to edit text fields. Sometimes I need to do some code changes for sites in a text box, or work with a large amount of text, and it was quite handy to simply edit with TextMate. It seemed to have broken in Leopard. I checked the settings, and changing the path to <pre>/usr/bin/mate</pre> from <pre>/usr/local/bin/mate</pre> seemed to do the trick. Might have just been me, but it’s working now, and I’m happy.
